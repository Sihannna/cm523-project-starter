About Project

Hi, I'm Annita Lin, a senior Media Science student at Boston University. Unlimited Workout is designed to make workout accessible to anyone, anywhere, at any time. With a vast library of instructional workout videos, my platform offers something for everyone. At Unlimited workout, I believe that workout is not just limited in gym, but a way of life.

Join me today and discover how my platform can help you lead a healthier, happier life, one workout at a time!
